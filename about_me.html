Facts About Me

BSIT was never really my choice. I wasn’t able to take Psychology, the dream I once held close, because my parents didn’t want that path. So I followed what they chose, instead of the life I imagined for myself. At home, I find comfort in isolation—lost in Wattpad and manga, where reality fades and I quietly slip into different worlds, where I can breathe a little easier. I love hiking, too. It reminds me that life can be exhausting and heavy on the way up, but when you finally reach the top, everything you endured suddenly feels worth it I love traveling because every new place makes me feel free, like I’m not trapped by anything for a while. I also love fashion—making myself wear different colors and styles because through that, I can express who I truly am. And most of all, I love photography. I want to own my own camera. I already bought a digital one, because when I want something, I work for it until it becomes mine—earned through effort and patience. Now, my goal is a Canon camera with a big lens, something that can capture the world the way I see it: raw, real, and alive.
